Case Study

First Social Commerce App in the MENA Region

Company
Pick App
Role
Product Design
Year
2024
Timeline
3 Months
Overview

Commerce meets content, for the first time in MENA.

PickApp is a social commerce platform transforming online shopping in Saudi Arabia by combining short-form videos and live commerce. It bridges the gap between content, community, and commerce — making product discovery engaging and interactive.

Before Pick App, there was no product in the MENA region that let creators sell directly through short-form video content. The challenge was designing an experience that felt as natural as scrolling TikTok, but with a seamless buying flow embedded throughout.

The Solution

Scroll. Discover. Buy. That simple.

PickApp integrates social engagement with e-commerce through three core experiences:

📱
Short-Form Video Shopping
Users discover products through engaging creator content — the TikTok model, built for Saudi commerce.
🔴
Live Commerce
Real-time interactions between sellers and buyers. Watch, ask, and buy — all in one stream.
✅
Seamless Checkout
Instant purchases without leaving the app. Zero friction from "I want this" to "it's mine."
Key Screens

Built screen by screen.

Home — Video Feed
Immersive product discovery inspired by TikTok's scroll experience — but built for shopping.
Interests Selection
Pick what you love. We personalize your feed with products and creators that match.
Onboarding Walkthrough
Reduces confusion and ensures users can navigate the app smoothly from day one.
Creator / User Profile
Influencers showcase their favorite products and drive sales through their video content.
Impact

Pick App launched as the first social commerce platform in the MENA region — a product category that didn't exist there before.
