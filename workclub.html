Case Study

WorkClub: Unlocking Flexible Workspaces On-Demand

Company
Workclub
Role
Product Design
Year
2021
Timeline
8 Months
The Problem

Finding flexible workspaces shouldn't be this hard.

Freelancers, remote workers, and startups in major cities struggle to find affordable, flexible, and productive workspaces. Traditional coworking spaces require costly memberships, while coffee shops often lack reliable WiFi, quiet spaces, and a professional environment. Workers needed a pay-as-you-go solution that gives access to high-quality workspaces without long-term commitments.

My Process

Research-first, design-second.

01
Research & Planning
  • Interviewed remote workers, freelancers & startup teams
  • Analyzed competitors: WeWork, Regus, traditional memberships
  • Studied the rise of remote & hybrid work
  • Built detailed user personas
02
Design & Prototyping
  • User flow mapping & journey maps
  • Low-fidelity wireframes for key screens
  • High-fidelity UI aligned with Workclub's brand
  • Interactive Figma prototype
03
Testing & Optimization
  • Usability testing with real users
  • A/B tested UI variations to optimize conversion
  • Detailed design specs for developer handoff
The Solution

An on-demand network of workspaces.

Workclub partners with underutilized venues — hotels, cafes, and coworking spaces — to create a flexible, affordable workspace network accessible through a simple app.

⚡
Access Instantly
No need for expensive memberships. Book a workspace on demand, in seconds.
💳
Flexible Pricing
Pay only for the time and services you actually need. No hidden fees.
🤝
Community & Networking
Connect with like-minded professionals in inspiring, productive spaces.
Key Screens

Designed for clarity at every step.

Map View
Discover available coworking spaces on a map. Search by location, apply filters, view details.
Workspace Details & Booking
Images, capacity, pricing, and location. Check availability and book in one tap.
Booking Confirmation
Booking details at a glance — date, time, duration, price. Invite guests and add to calendar.
Results

Workclub was acquired by Upflex after closing a $350K funding round — with thousands of satisfied users and a validated product.
